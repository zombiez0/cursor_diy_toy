📝 Paper Craft Tips for Success

📐 Choose the Right Paper

Use colored construction paper, origami paper, or even newspaper for different effects. Thicker paper works better for 3D projects.

✂️ Sharp Tools Matter

Child-safe scissors that cut cleanly make all the difference. Dull scissors can tear paper and frustrate kids.

👪 Make It Social

Paper crafts are perfect for family time. Work together and help younger children with more complex folds.

🎨 Add Personal Touches

Encourage kids to decorate their creations with markers, stickers, or glitter to make them unique.
