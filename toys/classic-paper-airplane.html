Begin with a standard 8.5x11 inch piece of paper. Place it on a flat surface with the long edge facing you (landscape orientation).

Fold the paper in half lengthwise (hot dog fold), bringing the long edges together. Press the crease firmly, then unfold.

ğŸ’¡ Pro Tip: This center crease is your guide line for all future folds.
3

Create the First Wing Folds

Fold the top corners down to meet the center crease line. You should now have a triangle shape at the top with a rectangular base.

ğŸ’¡ Pro Tip: Make sure both corners are folded at exactly the same angle for balanced flight.
4

Fold the Angled Edges Again

Take the angled edges you just created and fold them down to the center line again. This creates the airplane's nose and main body.

ğŸ’¡ Pro Tip: The nose should be sharp and pointed for the best aerodynamics.
5

Fold the Plane in Half

Fold the entire plane in half along the original center line. The folded edges should be on the outside.

ğŸ’¡ Pro Tip: Make sure the nose stays sharp and pointed after this fold.
6

Create the First Wing

With the plane folded in half, fold one side down to create the first wing. The wing should align with the bottom of the plane's body.

ğŸ’¡ Pro Tip: The wing should be perpendicular to the body for stable flight.
7

Create the Second Wing

Flip the plane over and fold the other side down to create the second wing. Make sure it matches the first wing exactly.

ğŸ’¡ Pro Tip: Both wings should be identical for balanced, straight flight.
8

Open the Wings

Carefully open the wings so they're perpendicular to the body. Make sure both wings are at the same angle.

ğŸ’¡ Pro Tip: The wings should be flat and level for the best flight performance.
9

Check Your Airplane

Inspect your airplane from the front, back, and sides. Make sure it's symmetrical and the wings are even.

ğŸ’¡ Pro Tip: Any unevenness will cause the plane to fly in circles or dive.
10

Test Flight!

Hold the airplane underneath the body, just behind the nose. Throw it gently forward with a smooth motion. Watch it soar!

ğŸ’¡ Pro Tip: Throw level and straight - don't throw it up or down at an angle.
