Create the classic paper fortune teller (also called a cootie catcher) that has entertained kids for generations! This interactive paper game predicts the future, answers questions, and provides endless entertainment for friends and family.

üìã Step-by-Step Instructions

1

Start with a Square

Begin with a square piece of paper. If you only have rectangular paper, fold one corner to the opposite edge and cut off the excess to make a perfect square.

üí° Pro Tip: Use colorful paper or white paper that you can decorate later.
2

Fold Diagonally Both Ways

Fold the paper in half diagonally to create a triangle, then unfold. Repeat with the other diagonal. You should have an X pattern of creases.

üí° Pro Tip: Press the creases firmly - they'll help guide your future folds.
3

Fold All Corners to Center

Fold each of the four corners to meet at the center point where the diagonal creases cross. You'll have a smaller square.

üí° Pro Tip: Make sure all corners meet exactly at the center point.
4

Flip and Fold Again

Flip the paper over and fold all four corners to the center again. You now have an even smaller square.

üí° Pro Tip: Keep the folds tight and precise for the best final result.
5

Fold in Half Both Ways

Fold the small square in half horizontally, then unfold. Fold in half vertically, then unfold. This creates the movement creases.

üí° Pro Tip: These creases will allow your fortune teller to open and close smoothly.
6

Insert Your Fingers

Flip the fortune teller over and gently push your thumbs and index fingers into the four pockets on the back.

üí° Pro Tip: Be gentle - the paper might tear if you're too rough.
7

Test the Movement

Practice opening and closing the fortune teller by bringing your fingers together and apart, then side to side.

üí° Pro Tip: The fortune teller should open in two different directions smoothly.
8

Add Colors to Outside

Color or decorate the four outside triangles with different colors. These will be the choices people pick from.

üí° Pro Tip: Use bright, easy-to-see colors like red, blue, green, and yellow.
9

Add Numbers Inside

Open the fortune teller and write numbers 1-8 on the inside triangles. These numbers will be used for counting.

üí° Pro Tip: Write the numbers clearly so they're easy to read during the game.
10

Write Fortunes

Lift up each numbered triangle and write a fortune or prediction underneath. Make them fun and positive!

üí° Pro Tip: Keep fortunes kid-friendly and fun: "You will have a great day!" or "A fun surprise awaits!"
11

Learn How to Play

Have someone pick a color, spell it out while opening/closing the fortune teller, then pick a number and count it out.

üí° Pro Tip: Practice the game yourself first so you can teach others how to play.
12

Reveal the Fortune

Lift up the final triangle to reveal their fortune! Watch their reaction and have fun with the predictions.

üí° Pro Tip: Make the fortune-telling dramatic and fun - it's all about the entertainment!
